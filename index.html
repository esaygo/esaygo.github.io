About us

Located just minutes away from downtown Seattle in Seward Park, Dinosaur Sanctuary provides a home for the remaining members of this species. The beautiful shores of Lake Washington serve as a perfect watering hole for this majestic species. Our non-profit seeks to ensure a safe home for these creatures which allows them the freedom to roam the forests and enjoy the cool lake waters. We hope that you will consider a visit to our sanctuary to experience these beauties before it's too late!

Features

The park offers a wide variety for young and old

  • Cross breeds

    • We have genetically engineered a cross breed of Artic penguin with a Microraptor. It is not only environmentally friendly, but it make also for a great pet :)

  • Kids activities

    • Kids can safely enjoy the company of baby dinosaurs. They are friendly and hand-reared

  • Dinosaurs feeding time

    • For a small domation you can feed the dinosaurs. Just make sure you sign the waiver first!
